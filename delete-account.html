Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Please read the information below before submitting your deletion request.

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be removed within 30 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your account profile and login credentials
  • Order history and delivery records
  • Uploaded payment receipts and media files
  • Quotation requests and chat history
  • Ledger and transaction records

📌 Some data may be retained for a limited period if required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., financial records).

How to request deletion

Send us an email from your registered email address with the subject line "Account Deletion Request". Include your full name and registered email in the message body.

  1. Tap the button below to open your email app
  2. Make sure you're sending from your registered email address
  3. We will verify your identity and process the request within 7 business days
  4. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted
📧 Send Deletion Request

Or email us directly at Idealsolespk@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request".
